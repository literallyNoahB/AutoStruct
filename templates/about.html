About AutoStruct

AutoStruct is a online web application for the elucidation of dry protein samples. The tool was developed by the Bioinspired Materials Laboratory at the University of Michigan run by Dr. Abdon Pena-Francesch. For inquiries about AutoStruct's specific methods please refer to our technical paper.
