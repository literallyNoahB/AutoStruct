About AutoStruct

AutoStruct is a online web application for the elucidation of dry protein samples. The tool was developed by the Bioinspired Materials Laboratory at the University of Michigan run by Dr. Abdon Pena-Francesch. For inquiries about AutoStruct please refer to the the publication or contact the Bioinspired Materials Lab.
